About the Club

Sacramento Brick Builders (SacBB) is a vibrant community for LEGO enthusiasts across the greater Sacramento region. We gather to build, trade pieces, showcase creations, and share our passion for the brick.

From first-time builders to experienced MOC creators, everyone is welcome. Whether you’re looking to get inspired, connect with fellow fans, or take part in public displays, there’s a place for you here.
